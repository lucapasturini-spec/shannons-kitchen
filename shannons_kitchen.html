Aloha, I'm Shannon!

I'm a home cook born and raised on O'ahu, with a deep love for Hawaiian flavors, family recipes, and feeding the people I care about.

What started as cooking for neighbors has grown into something special — a little kitchen business right here in Kailua, serving plates full of aloha to the community I love.

Every dish I make carries the spirit of the islands: fresh, warm, and made with real love. From classic plate lunches to local favorites, I put my heart into every bite.
